GBG is where we win as a team. The key is coordination: hit what‚Äôs called, don‚Äôt break locks, and don‚Äôt waste effort.

Manual-fight tips (simple)

  • Focus-fire dangerous units first (enemy artillery/ranged).
  • Don‚Äôt sacrifice wounded units ‚Äî keep them back.
  • Use cover/line-of-sight on tricky maps.
  • If your first two turns go bad, retreat and swap army.

Auto-battle safety tips

  • If you lose a unit twice in a row: consider stopping or going manual.
  • When fights start ‚Äúcosting‚Äù units, you‚Äôre near your safe attrition.
  • Once you are losing more than four units consistently, think about stopping.

Attrition Guide (When to Stop)

Attrition is personal. The goal is to farm points without feeding units. Your ‚Äúsafe‚Äù attrition depends on boosts, unit choice, and whether you manual-fight.

Easy rule of thumb

  • Keep going: you‚Äôre winning clean with minimal damage.
  • Slow down: units are taking heavy hits or you‚Äôre replacing losses.
  • Stop: stop before you start taking heavy losses.

Auto vs Manual

  • Auto: fast and consistent, but can throw units away at higher attrition.
  • Manual: takes longer, but pushes your safe attrition higher.
  • Best method: auto until fights get rough, then manual key matchups.
