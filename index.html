⚔️ Fighting Guide (By Age)

Expand your age to see recommended Attack and Defense combinations.

Iron Age (IA)▾

Attack combos

  • 1 Horse + 7 Rogues
  • 1 Legion + 7 Rogues
  • 2–4 Artillery + 4–6 Rogues

Defense combos

  • 8 Ballista
  • 6–7 Ballista + 1–2 Drummers
  • 8 Archers
  • 5 Drummers + 3 Rogues
Early Middle Ages (EMA)▾

Attack combos

  • 1 Horse + 7 Rogues
  • 2 Horse + 6 Rogues
  • 1 Heavy + 7 Rogues
  • 2–4 Artillery + 4–6 Rogues

Defense combos

  • 8 Catapults
  • 8 Mounted Archers
  • Drummers mixed with Rogues or Catapults (situational)
High Middle Ages (HMA)▾

Attack combos

Arrowheads notes HMA attack is “same as EMA/IRON”.

Defense combos

  • 8 Trebuchets
  • 6 Trebuchets + 2 Crossbows
Late Middle Ages (LMA)▾

Attack combos

Arrowheads notes LMA attack is “same as EMA/IRON”.

Defense combos

  • 8 Cannons
  • 8 Longbow Archers
  • Siege idea: 1 Cannon + 7 Rogues (can be surprisingly tough)
Colonial Age (CA)▾

Attack combos

Arrowheads notes CA attack is “same as EMA/IRON” (with Rangers hiding in trees).

Defense combos

  • 8 Field Guns
  • 1–2 Rangers + Rogues
  • 8 Muskets
  • 3 Heavy + 5 Rogues (and variations like 1 Heavy + 2 Rangers + 5 Rogues)
Industrial Age (Indy)▾

Attack combos

Arrowheads notes Indy attack is similar to EMA/IRON, with extra emphasis on Howitzers/Artillery.

Defense combos

  • 1–2 Jaegers + Rogues
  • 1 Jaeger + 1 Rifle + 6 Rogues
  • 8 Breech Loaders
  • 8 Rifles (best when mixed with other DAs)
Progressive Era (PE)▾

Attack combos

  • 4 Artillery + 4 Rogues
  • 2 Conscript + 6 Rogues
  • 1 Tank + 1 Conscript + 6 Rogues
  • 1 Sniper + 7 Rogues (situational)

Defense combos

  • 8 Artillery
  • 4 Conscript + 4 Sniper
  • 4 Artillery + 4 Sniper
Modern Era (ME)▾

Attack combos

  • 2–4 Artillery + 4–6 Rogues
  • 1 Heavy + 7 Rogues
  • 1 Fast + 7 Rogues

Defense combos

  • 8 Artillery
  • 8 Bazooka
  • 8 Paratrooper
Postmodern Era (PME)▾

Attack combos

  • 1–2 MG Team + Rogues
  • 2–3 Artillery + 5–6 Rogues
  • 1 Champs + 7 Rogues
  • 1 Fast + 7 Rogues

Defense combos

  • 8 Artillery
  • 8 Commandos (especially paired with 8 Artillery in other slots)
Contemporary Era (CE)▾

Attack combos

  • 1 Anti-Air + 7 Rogues (first move manual)
  • 2 Anti-Air + 6 Rogues
  • 2 Champs + 6 Rogues
  • 8 Helicopters
  • 1 Tank + 7 Rogues

Defense combos

  • 8 Assault Tanks
  • 7 Assault Tanks + 1 Missile
  • 8 Missiles (only when mixed with other DAs)
Tomorrow Era (TE)▾

Attack combos

  • 1–2 Champ + 6–7 Rogues
  • 1 Stealth Tank + 7 Rogues

Defense combos

  • 8 Stealth Tanks
  • 1–2 Stealth Tanks mixed with Rogues (annoying defense option)
Future Era (FE)▾

Attack combos

  • 2 Hover Tanks + 6 Rogues
  • 1 Rail Gun + 1 Hover Tank + 6 Rogues
  • 2 Rail Guns + 6 Rogues
  • 1 Hover Tank + 7 Rogues
  • 1 Satellite Spotter + 7 Rogues (situational)

Defense combos

  • 8 Hover Tanks
  • 3 Rail Guns + 5 Hover Tanks
  • 8 Drone Swarm (good to mix in a sector)
  • 8 Exoskeleton (best when not alone)
Arctic Future (AF)▾

Attack combos

(Not explicitly listed as combos on the Arrowheads attack page in the captured section.)

Defense combos

  • Include 1 flying unit (e.g., Dragon Drone) so artillery can’t hit everything
Oceanic Future (OF)▾

Attack combos

  • 2 Subcruisers + 6 Rogues
  • 1 Subcruiser + 7 Rogues
  • 2 Turtles + 2 Subcruisers + 4 Rogues (vs mixed turtles)
  • 8 Turtles (no flying units present)

Defense combos

  • Include 1 flying unit (e.g., Glider) so artillery can’t hit everything
Virtual Future (VF)▾

Attack combos

  • 1 Ronin + 7 Rogues
  • 2 Ronin + 6 Rogues
  • 2 Rockets + 2 Ronin + 4 Rogues (vs mixed rockets)
  • 8 VF Rockets (no flying units present)

Defense combos

  • Ninjas can be annoying when mixed (not as a single/only defense)
Space Age Mars (SAM)▾

Attack combos

  • 2 Wardens + 6 Rogues
  • 1 Sentinel + 1 Warden + 6 Rogues
  • 1–2 Sentinels + 6–7 Rogues
  • 4 Sentinels + 4 Rogues
  • 8 Sentinels (situational)

Defense combos

  • 7–8 Nail Guns (tough option mentioned for later ages; see SAAB note)
  • 7 Wardens + 1 Sentinel (noted as toughest city defense idea)
Space Age Asteroid Belt (SAAB)▾

Attack combos

  • 2 Nail Guns + Rogues
  • 4 Artillery + 4 Rogues (no fast/flying in enemy)
  • 8 Artillery (no flying in enemy)

Defense combos

  • 7–8 Nail Guns (toughest to kill)
  • Include 1 flying fast unit (to block rocket-style attacks)
Space Age Venus (SAV)▾

Attack combos

  • 2–3 Power Dragons + Rogues
  • 2–4 Fast + Rogues (or 8 Fast)

Defense combos

  • 8 Energy Cannons (fast) is noted as toughest defense
Space Age Jupiter Moon (SAJM)▾

Attack combos

  • 2 Harpoon Grenadiers + Rogues
  • 2–4 Fast + Rogues (or 8 Fast)
  • 8 Cavitation Tanks (no flying units present)

Defense combos

  • 8 Abyssal Gliders (fast)
  • 8 Harpoon Grenadiers (ranged, Contact skill)
  • Include 1 champ or flying fast unit (synergy note)
Space Age Titan (SAT)▾

Attack combos

  • 2–4 Fast + Rogues (or 8 Fast)
  • 2 Artillery + 2 Light + 4 Rogues (vs mixed artillery enemies)
  • 8 Artillery (no flying units present)

Defense combos

  • 8 Fast units (toughest defense)
Space Age Space Hub (SASH)▾

Attack combos

  • 2 Ranged + Rogues (Contact skill strategy)
  • 2–4 Fast + Rogues (or 8 Fast)
  • 2–3 Artillery + Rogues
  • Special: vs 4 Fast + 4 Ranged enemy → 2 Ranged + 1 Heavy + 5 Rogues

Defense combos

  • 8 Ranged units (Contact skill) – tough defense
  • 8 Fast units
  • 8 Artillery
